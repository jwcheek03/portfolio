About me

Who am I?

I am a BS in Computer Science (Game Development Concentration) at North Carolina State University with a minor in creative writing. I have a strong passion for storytelling and worldbuilding and explore that through my interests of theater, creative writing, and game development. I am seeking opportunities to apply my passions and creativity in a professional setting.

I have had many experiences to develop core professional skills such as leadership, communication, and conflict resolution. I am interested in pursuing experiences that will provide me with opportunities to test and develop these skills in a professional setting. Through my experience I have gained proficiency with Java, Python, C/C#, HTML/CSS, and learned game engines such as Unreal Engine, Unity, and Godot.
