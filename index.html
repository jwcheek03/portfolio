About me



Who am I?

I am John Cheek, a BS in Computer Science (Game Development Concentration) student at North Carolina State University with a minor in creative writing.

My interest in programming started in high school as a programmer for the Torch 5804 FIRST Robotics Team and decided I wanted to continue exploring that field in college. That, combined with my passion for video games and story telling has further driven me to pursue a career in game development. Since then, I have had many experiences to develop the necessary technical skills needed for such a career as well as pursue essential soft skills such as leadership, communication, teamwork, and conflict resolution.

While I have learned much through my classwork and gained proficiency with Java, C, and HTML/CSS; I have taught myself other languages such as C++, C#, and Python. Additionally, I have taught myself the basics digital design using tools like Blender and the Adobe Suite and learned how to develop games using popular engines such as Unreal Engine, Unity, and Godot.

I am an avid learner and constantly looking to expand my repertoire of software and game development tools.

I have a strong passion for storytelling and worldbuilding and explore that through my interests of theater, creative writing, and game development. I am actively seeking opportunities to apply my passions and creativity in a professional setting.
