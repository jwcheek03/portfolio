About me



Who am I?

I am John Wilson Cheek, a BS in Computer Science Game Development Concentration student at North Carolina State University with a minor in creative writing.

I have a strong passion storytelling, worldbuilding, and sharing my ideas with others which I explore through my interests of theater, creative writing, and game development. I am actively seeking opportunities to apply my passions and creativity in a professional setting.

My interest in programming started in high school as a programmer for the Torch 5804 FIRST Robotics Team which pushed me to continue exploring that field in college. That, combined with my passion for video games and story telling, has further driven me to pursue a career in game development.

Through my academic and extracurricular pursuits at NC State I have had many experiences to gain the necessary technical skills needed for such a career as well as develop essential soft skills such as leadership, communication, teamwork, and conflict resolution needed for the job.

While I have learned much through my classwork and gained proficiency with Java, C, and HTML/CSS, I have taught myself other languages such as C++, C#, and Python through my personal projects. I am an avid learner and constantly looking to expand my repertoire of software skills and game development tools. I am self-taught in the basics of digital design using tools like Blender and the Adobe Suite and have learned how to develop games in popular engines such as Unreal Engine and Godot Engine.

Writing

I have always had an interest in writing and pursuing a creative writing minor has helped me develop this interest and improve my writing skills. While I primarily write fantasy short stories, I enjoy exploring many different genres and mediums of storytelling. With the foundation of my writing, I can further realize the worlds and stories I write through tabletop role-playing games and the games I create.

I take pride in the fact I have never used Generative AI in my writing. While Generative AI is a very powerful tool and becoming an integral part of professional life, I strongly belive in the passion and authenticity behind fully human creative work.

King Solun Boss Fight Gameplay

At the end of their journey the player faces off against the spirit of King Solun. In this battle, he uses a projectile and summon attack.

Rebuild Mechanic Gameplay

When following the path of restoration, the player can rebuild ruined structures and return Solunaris to its former glory.

Attacking Combat Gameplay

When following the path of destruction, the player can attack to elimiate threats in their way.

Spirit World Portal Gameplay

Spirit World portals serve to refil the players HP and provide a glimpse into the past of Solunaris.

Game Info

  • Developed for PC in Godot Engine using a combination of GDScript and C#
  • Two unique paths the player can persue via engaging with the game world using different mechanics
  • Adaptive NPC dialogue that changes based on the players progression
  • Collaborated with a four-person team to identify target aesthetics, desired player experiences, and design the game's mechanics

Personal Contributions

  • Conceptualized the game’s narrative and wrote over 100 lines of story-related non-player character dialogue
  • Developed a dialogue system and UI from scratch that adapts to the player's actions and changes in the game world
  • Programmed the combat system, enemy AI, boss fight behavior, and spirit world portal interaction
  • Designed a tile set for the game world and created the game levels based on a diagram from the art team

Other Contributors

  • Artist/Writer: Aysatu Dialo, Programmer: Karthik Nandakumar, Artist: Judy Lin
